Introduction

About Me

I am an interdisciplinary engineering educator, AI researcher, and technologist with 15+ years of experience spanning academia and industry at the intersection of Electrical & Computer Engineering and Artificial Intelligence. Currently completing my Ph.D. at the University of Georgia (4.00 GPA), I bridge rigorous research and production-grade engineering to deliver scalable, high-impact solutions.

My technical expertise spans agentic AI, large language models, machine learning/deep learning, reasoning evaluation, semantic traceability, and neural circuit synthesis. I spearheaded the development of TAIWO and PEASSA — patent-pending prompt engineering frameworks that leverage LLMs for rigorous qualitative data analysis — and co-created CORE-Bench, a Google-selected benchmark advancing cross-model validation and robust reasoning assessment for complex AI systems.

In industry, I applied large-scale data analytics to industrial automation at Kubota R&D, optimizing testing cycles by 30% and improving data accessibility by 85% through analysis of over 600,000 dataset entries — demonstrating a proven ability to translate theory into measurable operational impact.

In academia, I advance engineering education through data-driven, mixed-methods research addressing the persistent "competency chasm" between engineering curricula and evolving workforce demands. I have led multi-state research initiatives and authored 4+ journal papers and 10+ peer-reviewed conference proceedings in venues including the Journal of Engineering Education, ACL, ASEE, IEEE FIE, and AERA. I also founded PURE-STEM, an undergraduate research mentorship platform that strengthens student research capacity through structured guidance, and have developed transparent assessment systems — including tailored grading rubrics and AI-assisted analytic workflows — to boost instructional clarity and learning outcomes.

Across both sectors, I am driven by building scalable, ethical, and high-impact AI and educational innovations — from intelligent systems that evaluate and improve model reasoning to evidence-based frameworks that align engineering training with real-world practice. I bring a rare combination of research depth, technical leadership, and practical implementation skill at the frontier of AI-enabled engineering practice and learning.
